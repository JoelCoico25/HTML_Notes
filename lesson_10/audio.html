Audio Handling in HTML

HTML provides the <audio> element to embed audio content in web pages. You can include controls for play, pause, and volume adjustment.

Example:

Attributes of the <audio> element:

  • controls: Adds play, pause, and volume controls.
  • autoplay: Starts playing the audio automatically (not recommended for user experience).
  • loop: Makes the audio loop continuously.
  • muted: Starts the audio in a muted state.
